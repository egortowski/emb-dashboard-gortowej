## Evidence Gaps and Limitations

### Missing Practitioner Perspectives
- **Failed Implementation Cases:** More detailed insight into why programs fail completely
- **Economic Stress Contexts:** Practitioners who succeeded during recessions or major cost pressures
- **Micro-Business Experience:** Practitioners from businesses with <20 employees
- **Employee-Turned-Manager:** Perspectives from those who experienced both sides of retention programs

### Critical Knowledge Gaps
- **Precise ROI Calculations:** More specific financial analysis of intervention costs vs. savings
- **Industry Customization:** Detailed adaptation guidance for different service industry segments
- **Technology Integration:** How modern scheduling and communication tools affect implementation

### Methodological Limitations
- **Self-Selection Bias:** Practitioners willing to share may not represent typical experiences
- **Success Story Focus:** Limited detail on partial failures or mixed outcomes
- **Geographic Concentration:** Primarily East Coast/Virginia perspectives may limit national applicability
