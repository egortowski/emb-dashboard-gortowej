## ROI Projection Analysis

### Solution Investment Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Basic Retention Program**
- **Annual Investment:** $15,000 (wage premiums + basic scheduling improvements)
- **Expected Turnover Reduction:** 25% (from 73% to 55%)
- **Annual Savings:** $28,800 (9 fewer replacements × $3,200 cost reduction)
- **Net ROI:** $13,800 annual benefit, 92% return on investment

**Scenario 2: Comprehensive Retention Program**  
- **Annual Investment:** $25,000 (wages + scheduling + training programs)
- **Expected Turnover Reduction:** 40% (from 73% to 44%)
- **Annual Savings:** $46,080 (14.4 fewer replacements × $3,200 cost reduction)
- **Net ROI:** $21,080 annual benefit, 84% return on investment

**Scenario 3: Premium Retention Program**
- **Annual Investment:** $35,000 (full compensation + benefits + development)
- **Expected Turnover Reduction:** 55% (from 73% to 33%)
- **Annual Savings:** $64,000 (20 fewer replacements × $3,200 cost reduction)
- **Net ROI:** $29,000 annual benefit, 83% return on investment
