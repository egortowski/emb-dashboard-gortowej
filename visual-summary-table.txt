│  🎯 STRONGEST FINDINGS:                                                                 │
│     • Retail & food service have 47-104% higher turnover than national average        │
│     • Both industries pay 32-41% below national median wages                          │
│     • Strong inverse relationship between wages and turnover across all industries     │
│                                                                                         │
│  📈 EMERGING TRENDS:                                                                    │
│     • Post-COVID demand for flexibility increased 41%                                 │
│     • Younger workers prioritize flexibility over compensation                         │
│     • Remote work options limited by customer-service nature of roles                 │
│                                                                                         │
│  🔍 DATA GAPS:                                                                          │
│     • Systematic tracking of flexible scheduling policies needed                       │
│     • Career development program measurement inconsistent                              │
│     • Establishment-level HR practice data limited                                     │
